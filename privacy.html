Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 17, 2026

Our promise

BabyZodiak — including the pregnancy planner, the zodiac compatibility tools, the live planetary almanac, and the Cosmic Trivia game — is designed to work entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal information. No account, no sign-in, no tracking.

Information we collect

None. The app does not collect any data about you or your device. Any date you enter (such as a pregnancy cycle start date used to estimate a baby's zodiac sign) is used only on your device to calculate the result and is not transmitted anywhere.

Network & third-party services

BabyZodiak does not make any network requests. It does not use analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or any third-party SDKs. Today's planetary positions are computed locally from astronomical formulas — no API calls.

Storage on your device

The app does not save the dates you enter between sessions. Closing the app discards any pregnancy or birth dates you've typed in.

Cosmic Trivia score history (games played, games finished, total correct answers, total wrong answers) is the one exception: it is stored locally on your device using the system's standard local preferences storage so the results screen can show your stats. This data never leaves your device, is never associated with an identity, and can be wiped at any time by deleting and reinstalling the app.

Children

The app is rated 4+ and does not target children. Because we collect no data from anyone, we do not knowingly collect data from children.

Entertainment only

BabyZodiak is provided for entertainment purposes only. It is not medical advice. For medical questions, please consult a qualified healthcare professional.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes in the future, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date. The current version always applies.
