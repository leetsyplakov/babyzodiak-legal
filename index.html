About

BabyZodiak helps you pick — or simply discover — your future baby's zodiac sign. Enter the first day of your last menstrual period and the app shows the sign your baby is on track to be, based on the standard 40-week pregnancy window. Or pick the sign (or several signs) you'd prefer and the app shows the cycle-start windows that would lead to each one.

Every sign comes with a baby-focused personality description, infant-temperament strengths and weaknesses, best and trickiest baby-parent and baby-sibling fit, and a library of 300+ historical and fictional baby/child bios showing how the sign revealed itself in their earliest years. Where the content touches developmental temperament, attachment, or sibling dynamics we cite the actual research (Thomas & Chess, Rothbart, Ainsworth, Aron, Brody, Dunn, Volling, Howe & Recchia).

The home screen also features a live planetary almanac — today's positions of the Sun, Moon, Mercury, Venus, Mars, Jupiter, and Saturn through the zodiac — computed entirely on your device with no network calls. Tap any planet+sign combination for a deeper read.

Pregnant or just curious? Play Cosmic Baby Trivia, a quick eight-question astrology quiz. Your game count, finished games, and correct/wrong tallies are stored locally on your device so you can watch your stats grow over time.

This app is for entertainment and planning purposes only and is not medical advice.
